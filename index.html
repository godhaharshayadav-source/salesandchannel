Setting the stage for our market strategy.

A global leader in visual solutions. In the UAE, we hold a 28% Market Share, positioning us as the aggressive Challenger to BenQ (35%). Our focus is high-quality, interactive collaboration tools.

1. Pricing: Education will demand ~10% volume discount.

3. On-Hold: 50% of On-Hold projects will resume within Q1-Q2.
